I work with new and early-stage entrepreneurs who need clarity, structure, and confidence in their business plans. My clients include:

  • First-time business owners who don’t know where to start
  • Entrepreneurs applying for loans, grants, or investor funding
  • People with strong ideas but difficulty organizing them
  • Founders who need a professional plan to move forward
  • Small business owners who want to refine or update an existing plan

Whether you're building a trucking business, a cleaning service, a food truck, or something entirely new, I help you turn your idea into a clear, actionable plan.
