Our Process

A clear, structured workflow designed to deliver lender‑ready business plans with confidence and professionalism.

Overview

Every engagement follows a structured 4–6 week process to ensure accuracy, clarity, and lender‑ready quality. This approach allows time for discovery, research, financial modeling, drafting, and revisions — resulting in a professional plan aligned with lender and regulatory expectations.

Timeline

Week 1 — Discovery & Intake

We begin by understanding your vision, goals, and operational concept. This includes clarifying whether the plan will support financing, licensing, investors, or internal planning.

Week 2 — Document Collection

You provide property details, staffing assumptions, financial inputs, and any relevant regulatory or licensing information. This stage ensures the plan is built on accurate, personalized data.

Week 3–4 — Research & Financial Modeling

Industry research, market analysis, competitive review, staffing structure, and multi‑year financial projections are developed based on your specific business model.

Week 4–5 — Drafting the Business Plan

A clear, structured, lender‑ready business plan is written, aligning narrative, financials, and operational details into a cohesive document.

Week 5–6 — Revisions & Finalization

We refine the plan based on your feedback or lender comments, ensuring the final document is polished, accurate, and ready for submission.

What You Can Expect

  • Clear communication throughout the engagement
  • Professional formatting and structured documentation
  • Realistic, defensible financial modeling
  • A predictable, organized workflow
  • A final plan aligned with lender and regulatory expectations

Optional Add‑Ons

Additional components can be added to support financing, pitching, or deeper analysis:

  • Executive summary refinement
  • Pitch deck outline or structure
  • Grant‑ready narrative sections
  • Market deep‑dive reports
  • Operational workflow diagrams
